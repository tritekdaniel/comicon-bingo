The Rules

  • When you find a cosplayer who matches a BINGO space, ask to take a photo of or with them
  • Tap the corresponding square to reveal the art and mark the space!
  • Once you get a BINGO, come back to the Cosplay Bingo Table in Cosplay Central for a prize!
  • Remain aware of your surroundings when asking for pictures. Don't block walkways, doorways, vendor booths and tables.
  • If a cosplayer says no, leave them be. Find a different cosplayer to fill the square!
  • Make sure not to bother anyone eating, taking a break, or clearly busy.
  • Each cosplay can only fill ONE square. (e.g. A Sailor Moon x Mandalorian mashup cosplay can fill EITHER a Sailor Moon square OR a Star Wars square OR a Mashup square)

Remember, Cosplay is not Consent! Always ask before taking a picture with someone.
